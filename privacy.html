Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we keep it safe.

1. What we collect

  • Account info: your name, email, and password (encrypted).
  • Your shop data: products, quantities, categories, and stock movements you create.
  • Technical info: basic logs (IP address, browser, errors) needed to keep the service running.

2. How we use it

  • To run the app and show you your data.
  • To send essential service emails (password resets, plan expiry).
  • To improve the app and fix bugs.

3. What we don't do

We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. We don't read your inventory.

4. Who can see your data

Only you. Each account is isolated using row-level security. Our team can only access your data with your explicit permission (e.g. when you ask for support).

5. Where it's stored

On secure cloud servers with encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Hosted by trusted providers (Supabase) with strict access controls.

6. Your rights

  • Export: Download your products as CSV anytime from the dashboard.
  • Delete: Ask us to delete your account and all your data — we'll do it within 7 days.
  • Correct: Update your name and email from Settings.

7. Cookies

We use a single session cookie to keep you logged in. No tracking cookies, no third-party ad cookies.

8. Children

SimpleStock is for shop owners and isn't directed at children under 13.
