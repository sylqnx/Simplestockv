FAQ

Quick answers

Is SimpleStock really free?
Yes. The Free plan lets you track up to 10 products forever. Upgrade only when you need more.
How much does Pro cost?
GH₵5 per week or GH₵20 per month. Pay via Mobile Money through WhatsApp — we activate within minutes.
Does it work offline?
Yes. SimpleStock installs to your home screen and the app loads instantly, even without internet. Changes sync when you reconnect.
Can I install it like a real app?
Yes. On Android, tap "Install" when the banner appears. On iPhone, tap Share → "Add to Home Screen".
Do I need internet to sign up?
You need internet for the first sign-up and to sync data. After that, the app loads instantly even on slow MTN or Vodafone connections.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Your data is private and only visible to your account. We use bank-level encryption and never share or sell your information.
What kind of shops use SimpleStock?
Provision stores, pharmacies, boutiques, hardware shops, salons, mini-marts — any shop with products to track.
